Heatwave Code of Conduct

The Heatwave is the 1st official supporters group for Tampa Bay Sun FC

We are committed to providing a fun, safe, environment for all Tampa Bay Sun FC fans/attendees:

  • You are encouraged to cheer, sing, wave flags, and otherwise support Tampa Bay Sun FC while remaining respectful and courteous to their fellow patrons, referees, opposing team fans and players.
  • No sexist, racist, misogynistic, homophobic, or transphobic language or behavior is welcome in our section.
  • Alcoholic beverages are consumed in a responsible manner and only by those of legal age.
  • If you have any questions or issues, please join our discord or send us an email at theheatwavesg@gmail.com
